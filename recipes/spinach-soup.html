Good to Know
Tips & Substitutions
Substitution

If you can't use spinach, you can substitute butter leaf lettuce. If you can't use tomatoes, substitute ripe mango. If neither works, blend banana with greens instead ‚Äî just don't include both banana and tomato, as they don't digest well together.

Cucumber Noodles

English cucumbers are a fun option when making noodles because of their small seeds. When using organic cucumber, you can leave the skin on. With conventional, peel first and discard skins.

Day 9 Note

If you're having Spinach Soup on Day 9 of the Simplified Cleanse, leave out the cucumber noodles. You can blend cucumber into the soup instead ‚Äî stick to juiced or blended foods only that day.

Enjoy It Raw

Make sure to enjoy the Spinach Soup raw rather than warming it up. The healing benefits of the fresh ingredients are preserved when consumed unheated.
